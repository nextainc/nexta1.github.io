Changing the middle class
Into a class

What was the entrepreneurial dream of two students of Sambalpur University, Manas Ranjan Panigrahi and Prabhat kumar Sahu is well on its way to become India’s next million-dollar start-up. 2 years ago, who could have thought that buying most advanced tech product in most affordable price would become a custom but NEXTA changed the demographics when it entered the market in 2017..

At the time of initial launch, Nexta Group was not a tech based company and was tied up with some local SMEs for fulfilling its customer requirements. Nexta Group became a Tech based company in 2017. It now has a channel partner network of over 31 branches across India, Nexta Group is able to offer Tech products like Inverter LED bulb, Speakers, Robotics equipment, Educational products and blind stick of Nexta products in 250+ Indian cities and towns, and has its own supply Hubs in 30 cities. Changing the way India shops for electronics product was not an easy task but NEXTA took up the challenge and came on top. Cashing in the strategy to ‘Let’s turn the middle class into a class’, Nexta has been positive in the last two quarters (Q2 and Q3) of 2018 and has plans to grow 100 per cent revenue in FY19.
