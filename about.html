Our Vision

Nexta.The biggest technical revolution of odisha ,the hope of every middle class family

Started by two college mates Manas Ranjan Panigrahi and Prabhat Kumar Sahu, an Odisha based startup in Sambalpur district of Odisha on Feb 21, 2017 as an e-commerce android app called NEXTAone in Sambalpur and in Burla on Dec 25, 2017 for the first time.

Nexta Group is a startup which is growing rapidly. Now 21 Year old,M anas Ranjan Panigrahi,Founder and CEO of NEXTA began his journey in Feb. 2017,starting with an ecommerce platform called Nextaone at Odisha, the company as of April 2018 established in 19 states across the country and ready to launch in other countries. It has been named Odisha’s largest SME’s E-commerce chain and an emerging products based company of this country. In Nexta in past we launched Vision( also known as infinity utterance), the Smart Blind stick for blind person by which they can feel the things near them. In this year we are going to LEAFY series.
